# Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This repository contains an **interactive business intelligence dashboard** designed to analyze **sales, shipments, profitability, and performance trends** across products, geographies, and sales personnel.

The dashboard provides a consolidated view of **current year (CY) vs previous year (PY)** metrics, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions related to revenue growth, logistics efficiency, and profit optimization.

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Sales Trend Analysis (CY vs PY)

* Line chart comparing **monthly sales amount** between the current year and previous year.
* Helps identify seasonality, growth patterns, and revenue dips.

### 2. Boxes Sold Trend (CY vs PY)

* Visual comparison of total boxes sold over time.
* Useful for tracking volume growth independent of revenue.

### 3. Shipment Distribution by Box Count

* Histogram showing **shipment count grouped by box quantity bins**.
* Highlights logistics patterns and shipment size distribution.

### 4. Top 6 Products by Sales

* Treemap visualization representing the **highest-performing products**.
* Quick identification of revenue-driving SKUs.

### 5. Product Performance Table

* Detailed table including:

  * Product Name
  * Total Sales Amount
  * Profit Percentage
* Conditional formatting to highlight high-margin products.

### 6. Salesperson Performance

* Ranked table of top salespersons including:

  * Total Sales Amount
  * Total Boxes Sold
  * Profit Percentage
* Performance indicators (icons) for quick assessment.

### 7. Geographic Revenue Distribution

* Donut chart showing **sales contribution by country**:

  * India
  * New Zealand
  * Australia
  * USA
  * UK
